Define the Problem

100 million of active uers learning and using technical analysis, but only 1% of traders publish their trading ideas.
Why?
The reason is that technical analysis is manual and time-consuming. The existing technical analysis tools can be hard to approach for the most of people.

Do you know how much code is needed to find the "W" shape pattern in stock?

200 lines of proprietary codes

Solutions & Goals

I worked with a team of product designers, product managers, researchers, and engineers. By building a responsive website experience for the combination of signal processing and image-recognition AI methods to provide the “Search by Drawing,” “Match by Symbol,” “Easy Start,” and “Historical and Cross-Asset Pattern Match” features.
We hope to:
01
Make it Easier
Create a professional, trustworthy, and user friendly experience with the AI matching and searching function for stock traders.
02
AI Engine & Samrt
Provide draw to search and match by symbol methods to help users find matching trend pattern stocks.
03
Implements & Improvements
Increase search result accuracy and reduce 8,000 minutes of manual work into 3 seconds.
04
Explore
Explore various revenue generation opportunities and convert more users to paying customers using the paid service to connect with our real-time data.

User Testing

We conducted usability testing with 15 users with the goal to find out:

  • What needs and goals drive people to use stock screener services?
  • What are people struggling with while using the current technical analysis tools?
  • How can we provide a better stock search engine for stock traders?
  • How can we build trust with customers?
Sythesis & Analysis

During the synthesis of our findings, we recognized some common pain points users experienced when using the current stock screeners(other platforms) and compare them with the WeWave MVP-A product.
